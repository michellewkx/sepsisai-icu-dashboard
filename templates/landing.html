Core Capabilities

Intelligent Sepsis Management from Detection to Treatment

A comprehensive AI-driven framework that supports clinicians at every stage of sepsis care with real-time analytics and actionable insights.

Robust Early Prediction

Perception Layer: Two-stage learning (cross-domain pre-training + clinical fine-tuning) overcomes data scarcity, achieving 4–8 hour early warning lead time.

Sparse-Aware Multimodal Fusion

Masking mechanism encodes missingness as clinical intent (MNAR). Cross-modal attention integrates EHR, clinical notes, and imaging for full-spectrum observability.

Disease Trajectory Modeling

Understanding Layer: TimeAttention-based latent state modeling captures the full pathological cascade from infection to multi-organ failure.

Uncertainty-Aware Forecasting

Quantile regression generates full probability distributions of future states, highlighting tail-risk scenarios for proactive ICU resource orchestration.

Decision Support & Explainability

Decision Layer: Dynamic risk arbitration with SHAP attribution and LLM-assisted clinical narratives bridges the "last-mile gap" from prediction to action.

Heavy Edge Deployment

"Heavy Edge, Non-Invasive" architecture: on-premise edge server with read-only HIS/EMR integration, zero data exfiltration, compliant with China's Data Security Law.

Workflow

How Sepsis Navigator Works

A three-layer closed-loop system: from raw clinical data to actionable medical decisions.

1

Perception Layer

Robust early prediction via two-stage pre-training, sparse-aware masking, and multimodal fusion of EHR + notes + imaging.

2

Understanding Layer

TimeAttention-based latent state trajectory modeling with uncertainty-aware quantile forecasting for disease progression.

3

Decision Layer

Dynamic risk arbitration with confidence calibration, SHAP attribution, and LLM-assisted clinical narrative generation.

4

Clinical Delivery

Dual-terminal alerts via EMR + DingTalk/WeChat with closed-loop feedback for continuous site-specific calibration.
